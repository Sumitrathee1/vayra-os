# Lead Management System

## Department

Client Management

## Owner

Sales Department

## AI Agent Responsible

Sales Manager AI

## Mission

The Lead Management System is responsible for capturing, organizing, qualifying, and converting potential clients into paying customers through a standardized and transparent sales process.

The objective is to ensure that every lead is handled professionally, consistently, and efficiently while maximizing conversion rates and maintaining an excellent customer experience.

---

# Scope

The Lead Management System manages:

- Lead Collection
- Lead Qualification
- Client Communication
- Discovery Meetings
- Requirement Collection
- Proposal Preparation
- Quotation Coordination
- Follow-up Activities
- Sales Pipeline Management
- Lead Conversion Reporting

---

# Objectives

- Never lose a lead.
- Respond to every enquiry professionally.
- Qualify every lead before preparing quotations.
- Collect complete project requirements.
- Maintain accurate client records.
- Increase lead conversion rate.
- Reduce quotation turnaround time.
- Create a smooth onboarding experience.

# Responsibilities

The Lead Management System shall:

1. Register every new lead.
2. Assign a Lead ID.
3. Record contact details.
4. Record project information.
5. Evaluate project suitability.
6. Schedule discovery meetings.
7. Create requirement summaries.
8. Recommend suitable design packages.
9. Coordinate quotation preparation.
10. Track sales progress.
11. Maintain follow-up schedules.
12. Update lead status.
13. Generate sales reports.

# Quotation Policy

Residential Design Charges

₹50 / Sq.Ft.

Payment Terms

50% Advance

50% Before Final Delivery

Refund Policy

5-Day Concept Validation Period

If cancellation occurs within 5 calendar days:

- Full refund if work has not started.
- Refund after deducting completed work if design work has begun.

After 5 days:

Advance payment becomes non-refundable.
