# Purpose

The Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) defines the type of client VAYRA Atelier is built to serve.

The purpose of this document is to ensure every department focuses on attracting, qualifying, and serving clients who align with the company's expertise, pricing, and business model.

Every Marketing, Sales, and Finance decision should be validated against this profile.
