# Success Criteria

A proposal is considered complete only when:

✓ Client requirements are fully understood.

✓ Scope is clearly defined.

✓ Pricing is accurate.

✓ Payment terms are transparent.

✓ Refund policy is explained.

✓ Deliverables are listed.

✓ Timeline is realistic.

✓ Proposal has passed quality review.

Only then may the proposal be sent to the client.
