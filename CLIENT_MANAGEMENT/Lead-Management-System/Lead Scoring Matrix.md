# Purpose

The Lead Scoring Matrix is used to evaluate every new enquiry based on predefined business criteria.

The objective is to:

- Prioritize high-value leads.
- Improve conversion rates.
- Reduce time spent on unqualified enquiries.
- Help the Sales Team focus on clients with the highest probability of conversion.

Every lead must be scored before a proposal or quotation is prepared.

# AI Decision Rules

If Score ≥ 90

Priority = Critical

Notify CEO

Prepare Proposal Immediately

---

If Score 80–89

Priority = High

Prepare Proposal

Assign Sales Follow-up

---

If Score 65–79

Priority = Medium

Request Missing Information

Schedule Discovery Meeting

---

If Score 50–64

Priority = Low

Keep Lead Active

Collect Additional Information

---

If Score < 50

Priority = Very Low

Politely decline or nurture for future opportunities

---

# Automatic Alerts

Notify CEO if:

- Score ≥ 90
- Design fee exceeds ₹75,000
- VIP or referral client
- Repeat client
- Urgent project (less than 15 days)

---

# Reporting Metrics

Track Monthly:

- Average Lead Score
- Number of A+ Leads
- Number of A Leads
- Number of Won Leads
- Number of Lost Leads
- Conversion Rate by Score
- Revenue by Lead Category

---

# Success Criteria

The Lead Scoring Matrix is successful when:

✓ Every enquiry receives a score.

✓ High-value leads are prioritized.

✓ Low-quality leads are filtered appropriately.

✓ Sales effort aligns with conversion potential.

✓ Lead score is recorded in the CRM.
