# Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) defines the complete sales process followed by VAYRA Atelier, from receiving a new enquiry to handing over a confirmed project to the Project Management Department.

The objective is to ensure every lead receives a consistent, professional, and timely experience.

---

# Scope

This SOP applies to:

- Residential Interior Design Projects
- Online Consultations
- Single Room Design Projects
- Complete Home Interior Design Projects

# Stage 1 — Lead Registration

## Objective

Record every enquiry received.

### Responsible

Sales Manager AI

### Inputs

- Client enquiry
- Contact details
- Source

### Actions

- Generate Lead ID
- Create Lead Record
- Record enquiry date
- Record source
- Assign status "New"

### SLA

Within **30 minutes** during business hours.

### Output

Lead Qualification Form

---

# Stage 2 — Initial Contact

## Objective

Establish first communication.

### Responsible

Sales Manager AI

### Actions

- Send welcome message
- Introduce VAYRA Atelier
- Thank the client
- Schedule discovery meeting
- Request floor plan and reference images

### Documents Requested

- Floor Plan
- Site Photos
- Property Details
- Reference Images

### SLA

Within **2 hours** of enquiry.

### Output

Discovery Meeting Scheduled

# Escalation Rules

Escalate immediately if:

Client requests major scope changes.

Budget differs significantly from project scope.

Payment delays exceed 7 days.

Communication stops for 14 days.

Client raises a formal complaint.
